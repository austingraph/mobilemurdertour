Honeycomb Hills is a mature, well-established community that has everything from small ranch homes to large country estates capable of keeping horses. There are beautiful Hill Country views of rolling hills full of elm, live oak, and cedar trees. There is plenty of wildlife in the area with the Balcones Canyonlands National Wildlife Refuge just down the road. There are still home sites and farms for sale in Honeycomb Hills, so it’s a great opportunity to design the custom home of your dreams. Honeycomb Hills students are served by the  Leander Independent School District. They attend Bagdad Elementary School, Leander Middle School, and Leander High School.

Honeycomb Hills is a friendly community and they have an active, organized Neighborhood Watch program.
