This home is currently under contract, but we are taking back-up offers.
From the moment you start to drive up into Honeycomb Hills, you know this neighborhood is going to be special. It does not disappoint. As you tun into the property, a private tree-lined drive takes you back to the estate home nestled into the back of this 2+ acre site. You feel like you are on an even bigger acreage because you can see no other homes and the Balcones Canyonlands Preserve is to the south and west. Even though you feel like you are way out in the country at a private, luxury lodge; groceries, services, and restaurants are only minutes away and all within a great school district.
Both Leander, Cedar Park are growing rapidly and have a rapidly growing list of excellent shopping, services, medical care, and more. The Cedar Park and Leander area already have a Randall’s, HEB Plus, Whole Foods 360, and Natural Grocer for groceries. Many, many close choices for medical care, vision care, dental services and chiropractic care, etc. Cedar Park has a large regional hospital and Leander just broke ground on a new St David’s Hospital. Leander has an Emergency Room minutes away at Lakeline and Crystal Falls Parkway.

Leander ISD is a great school district. Students living in this community attend Bagdad Elementary School, Leander Middle School, Tom Glenn High School. 

If you love nature and privacy, then this is THE home for you. Imagine waking up to and enjoying the cheerful choir of birds singing as you sip your morning coffee or herbal tea on the large screened in patio or covered back porch. 
This property was originally 6.6 acres in size, but 4.44 acres were sold and added to the Balcones Canyonlands Preserve to maintain the natural condition of the land.
This 3 bedroom home has 2 and a 1/2 baths. Built with Universal Design, this home is ADA accessible. Even the powder room is large enough for a wheel chair. There is a large roll in shower with fold up shower seat in the master bathroom.
