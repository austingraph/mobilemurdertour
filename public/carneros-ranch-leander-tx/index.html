Carneros Ranch is a new home community in Leander, Texas with easy access to Highway 183/183A. Carneros Ranch is located near shopping, dining, and parks in the rapidly growing Leander area. Residents have access to a community pool. The nearby Capital Metro Station provides an easy commuter rail alternative to driving into Austin.

Wilshire Homes and Lennar are the builders in Carneros Ranch.

Benbrook Ranch Park and Southwest Williamson County Regional Park offer a wide range of outdoor recreation including: disc golf, softball, soccer fields, playscapes, and trails. Fishing, non-motorized boating, and wildlife observation are available at the 45-acre Divine Lake Park.

NextGen designs by Lennar make it easier for you to share your home with older parents or adult children. NextGen homes often come with a separate garage, kitchenette, living room, bedroom, and bath suite for some privacy from the main home.

Bagdad Elementary, Leander Middle School and Tom Glen High School are the Leander ISD schools that service the students in this neighborhood.
