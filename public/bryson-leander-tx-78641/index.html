Bryson – Leander TX 78641

Bryson is a new home neighborhood in growing Leander Texas in Williamson County, just north of Austin. Located just north of the Leander TOD where Austin’s Capital Metro runs its commuter rail and bus service into Austin Texas, Bryson offers a beautiful streetscape with several very different home looks – kind of a take on modern Texas farmhouses! There is a Bryson Community Home Owners Association which is mandatory to be a member of. You will love the Bryson Community amenities which include a community pool, clubhouse, jogging, wheeling or walking path, play grounds and more. There are several new home builders building new homes in the Bryson Community. New Home plans include Farmhouse Style Plan 5523. Prices for new homes in the new Bryson community are ranging from the $300’s to the $500’s. Streets names in the new home community of Bryson include Chitalpa Street, Germander Road and others. US postal mail service in the Bryson neighborhood is in cluster mailboxes.

Bryson – Leander TX SCHOOLS

Children living in homes in the new home community of Bryson attend Leander ISD public schools or a private school of their choice. Jim Plain Elementary at 501 South Brook Drive in Benbrook Ranch would be the elementary school in Leander ISD for children of elementary age. Soon Austin Community College will build nearby in the Leander TOD.
