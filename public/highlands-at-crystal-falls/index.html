Highlands at Crystal Falls

The Highlands is one of the five communities in the 5,000-acre Crystal Falls development in Leander, Texas. Some of the Highlands’ best features include spacious lots, fantastic views of the Texas Hill Country, and quiet streets with large trees and beautiful landscaping.

The Highlands has an amazing new amenities center featuring a junior Olympic pool, kids’ spray and play area, disc golf course, and a fishing lake with a fountain. Home owners here also enjoy the Crystal Falls Golf Course, a challenging 18-hole course. The nearby amenities center in The Boulders features a grotto-style pool with waterfalls. Tennis courts, playgrounds, and hiking and biking trails are all available to residents of The Highlands.

Leander ISD serves the students in the Highlands. Winkley Elementary School is centrally located in The Highlands at Crystal Falls. It’s a convenient walk for school-aged children.

David Weekly Homes, Mercedes Homes, Perry Homes, Drees Custom Homes, and Highland Homes are all builders in the Highlands. Homes in the Highlands range in price from the high $200s to the mid $500s and are between 2,500 and 4,000 square feet in size whether they are new construction or resale.
