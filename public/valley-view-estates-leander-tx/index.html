Valley View Estates – Leander TX

Valley View Estates is a small acreage neighborhood on the southeast side of Leander, Texas just off of Ronald Reagan Boulevard and County Road 177. Horses are allowed so some homes have paddocks, loafing sheds and barns with stalls for horses. Valley View Estates is small, composed of just 2 streets, Valley View Circle and Valley View Drive.  With this south east side of Leander position, Valley View Estates home owners can get to Georgetown, Cedar park or Round Rock easily from this area. The back side of the lots on Valley View Drive back to beautiful Brushy Creek. Recreational, shopping and eating out activities abound from this area. Cedar Park’s 1890 Ranch is just a few short minutes away as well as the Williamson County Regional Park on County Road 175 or Sam Bass.
